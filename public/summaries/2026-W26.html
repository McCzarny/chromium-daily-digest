Overview

This week in Chromium, major architectural modernizations occurred across the tab strip, browser action frameworks, and HTML menu elements. Security and network safety were advanced with multi-networking support and rigorous spanification efforts. In total, 2601 relevant commits out of 3515 total across 7 days were processed.
