Overview

This week's updates focus on raising macOS deployment targets, strengthening sandboxing and memory safety, and executing key API and architectural deprecations across Windows and macOS platforms. A total of 32 relevant commits out of 3415 total across 7 days are highlighted.

Security & Process Isolation

    SecurityCrypto
  • ⚠️BREAKING CHANGE: Systematic conversion of non-fatal DCHECK macros to fatal CHECK assertions within content/browser/renderer_host continues, which may increase production crash rates for custom embedders if they hit previously unasserted paths. (e8c46f0) (156705b)
