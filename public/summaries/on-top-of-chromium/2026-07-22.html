Security & Identity

  • Enforced strict security level verification in WebAuthn Signal API operations on desktop platforms, blocking websites with invalid or untrusted TLS certificates. (a9f248b)
    SecurityCrypto

    SecurityStorage

    BluetoothmacOSSecurity

    TabsUIBrowser

    UImacOSMedia

    UIBrowserBuild

Networking & Protocols

    NetworkEnterprise
