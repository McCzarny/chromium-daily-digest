Chromium Weekly: 2026 Week 28

Overview

During Week 28 of 2026, Chromium developers focused on extensive architectural refactoring, platform security hardening, and transitioning the browser UI away from global singletons. Out of 642 total commits, 458 relevant commits out of 642 total across 7 days were processed for desktop embedders on Windows and macOS. Major changes include the enforcement of typed process identifiers, stricter network and USB security, and modernized UI interface architectures.
