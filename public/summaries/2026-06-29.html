Security & Network Hardening

  • Enhanced Privacy Hardening (DBSC) in CorsURLLoader by clearing device_bound_session_usage for opaque or CORS-filtered responses to prevent cross-origin leaks. (fbce76f)

    SecurityNavigationPayments
  • Introduced a deep-copy mechanism for IDataObject::SetData on Windows to mitigate use-after-free vulnerabilities when handling external data objects. (4f939d4)
    SecurityWindows

Feature Evolution & AI

    UIAccessibility

    AIAutofillPayments
